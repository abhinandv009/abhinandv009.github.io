I'm Abhinand V, an aspiring AI and game developer based in Kerala, India. I hold a Master of Computer Applications from Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, where I focused on machine learning, computer vision, and software engineering.

My work lives at the intersection of two passions: building intelligent systems that process and understand the real world, and crafting immersive game experiences that pull players into richly designed virtual ones.

On the AI side, I specialize in CNN-based architectures for medical imaging — my brain tumor detection model ensemble reached 97% accuracy across 12 architectures. On the game side, I'm now into Godot 4, building a football quiz game (FootIQ) and a full narrative football simulation with live world ticks and media feeds.

I believe the best software feels inevitable — engineered with precision, but designed with empathy.
